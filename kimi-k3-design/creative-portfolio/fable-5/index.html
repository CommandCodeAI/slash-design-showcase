( About )

Designer’s eye, engineer’s hands

Naymur

I’m Naymur — a frontend developer and creative working at the seam where design intent becomes shipped code. No hand-offs, no translation loss: the person who obsesses over the kerning is the same one writing the components.

My work lives in the details most people feel but never notice — easing curves, load sequences, the weight of a hover. I build with React, TypeScript and Next.js, and I share the craft with a growing audience on YouTube.

( Process )

Four moves, zero guesswork

01 / Discover

Listen first

A working session to map your goals, audience and taste. We leave with a shared brief and a clear definition of “done”.

02 / Design

Direction, then detail

Moodboards and type studies narrow into one confident direction. Every screen designed at real content, real breakpoints.

03 / Build

Code with intent

The design rebuilt as fast, accessible, motion-rich frontend. Weekly staging links — you watch it come alive.

04 / Ship

Launch and sharpen

Performance passes, QA across devices, analytics wired in. Then a calm handover with docs you’ll actually read.
